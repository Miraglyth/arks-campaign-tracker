About

Wot dis?

PSO2:NGS ARKS Campaign Tracker (ACT) is an all-in-one tracker of campaigns in the Global server for Phantasy Star Online 2: New Genesis. It consolidates all campaigns announced on the game's website, Twitter, YouTube, any other sources or simply not announced at all, into a clear listing.

Campaigns are organised by their status and sorted into a logical order for each: Campaigns that have Ended are listed by which ended most recently, those that are Active by when they will end, and those which are Upcoming by when they will start.

Campaigns can be clicked to see a detail view, and marked off as "Done" to help keep track of what's outstanding.

How dis?

ARKS Campaign Tracker stores no data on a server and uses no accounts. It saves campaign data and settings in your browser's storage which is used and updated as needed to track campaigns across visits.

All campaign data and settings can be deleted by clicking the button below.
